Why AllyTree
Built for allied health. By allied health.

Allied health students in Pakistan have always been underserved when it comes to quality, free, curriculum-aligned study material. AllyTree exists to fix that.

🆓

Always Free

Every resource — notes, MCQs, practical books, viva prep — is completely free. No subscription. No paywall. Ever.

🎓

UHS Aligned

All content is built around the exact UHS B.Sc. syllabus. Only what you actually need for your exams and vivas.

🧠

Practice On-Site

MCQ banks built directly into the platform. Solve questions, get instant feedback, retry wrong answers — no downloads.

🤝

1-on-1 Guidance

Book a free personal session with a senior student who has been through the same rotations, exams, and vivas.

📱

Mobile Friendly

Study on any device — phone, tablet, or laptop. AllyTree is fully responsive so you can revise anywhere, anytime.

🌱

Always Growing

New content added regularly. More subjects, more semesters, more departments — AllyTree grows with you.

Our Mission

Allied health students deserve better resources.

In Pakistan, allied health professionals — lab technologists, radiographers, OT technologists — are the backbone of the healthcare system. Yet they receive the least support when it comes to quality education materials.

AllyTree was built to change that. Starting from a single student at DGKMC who decided to build the resource he wished existed, AllyTree is growing into a platform for every allied health student in Pakistan.

Free knowledge. No white coat required.
